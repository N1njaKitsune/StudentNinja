Mushin · The Still Mind
No mind — no ego — no hesitation
In Japanese martial arts, Mushin — empty mind — is the state where thought and hesitation fall away. The ninja acts without ego, reads without judgement, breathes without force. Through practice, the mind becomes like water: calm, deep, and unstoppable.
